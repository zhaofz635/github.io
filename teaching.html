  • September 2016, Course: Introduction to Physics (Undergraduate), Jilin University, China
    • Course Description: The introduction to physics course prioritizes knowledge transmission with clear goal setting. The Tyler model focuses on achieving these goals and is ideal for this course.

  • September 2014, Course: Evolution of Automotive Technology (Undergraduate), Jilin University, China
    • Course Description: The evolution of automotive technology follows a clear historical progression. The linear sequence model is well-suited for the chronological presentation of course content.
    • Design: Present the development of automotive technology in chronological order, from steam cars to modern electric vehicles, and discuss technological advancements step-by-step.
